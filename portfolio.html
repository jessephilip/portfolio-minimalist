Web Developer

Hi! I'M JESSE MATHERNE

AND I AM A FULL STACK WEB DEVELOPER.


Welcome to my portfolio. As you look around, you will see what I am passionate about. Coding. I have been coding, off and on for nearly twenty years. Unfortunately, most of that time was off, but recently, I decided to throw my plans to the wind and pursue this lifetime hobby of mine as my profession. I put down the law books, gave up my pursuit of the gavel, and entrenched myself in the ever-evolving life of a developer.

I'm slated to complete UT's Coding Boot Camp in about a month, and I have many different projects currently in the works. You can find most of them on my Github account. Feel free to Fork any of them. That'd be awesome.

I'll throw some flare in here as well, so that anyone visiting here will not only get to meet Me the Developer, but Me the Person as well. Hope to talk to you soon. Have fun!!!
